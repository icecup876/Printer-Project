Homepage and Product page references

These are the references that were taken for both Homepage and product page. Firstly we have apple, the reference of Apple's website was taken because it had a navbar which looked simple and to the point. Next up, walmart, the reference of walmart was taken because of the way they displayed their products which are in small sections, they looked very inviting so reference of walmart was taken to make the product card in homepage. Finally, Hewlett-Packard's printer webiste the reason for this being taken as a reference is similar to walmart's website.

About us page and Contact page references



Minimalistic design was our priority as complexity of the website often leads to bad user experience. Canela's website was very similar to our idea so we chose it as a reference for the layout of 'about us' page. A short portfolio of members and their picture side by side makes the webpage look simple and organized. We added a box shadow around the information box to give it a more polished look.
